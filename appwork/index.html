Our Mission

To bring a basic education to those who don't have access to it, in the most rural sectors of India, through the use of modern yet subtly simple technologies. we want to help improve the lives of those who live in villages, orphans on the railway stations, and millions of other children who have almost lost hope.

group

One Platform

We found a single, flexible solution, to fit all users needs: a simple raspberry pi implementation that can play educational content- videos, audio, text- approved by the NCERT, on monitors across India in a secure and safe manner. Kids don't have to ask for an education- we bring the education to them!

settings

Implementation

Your preprepared content will be transfered onto an SD card, that can be plugged into any of our raspberry pi devices, preloaded with custom OS systems, they will play your content utilizing a specific classification system on monitors across India, in multiple languages and across many grades and subjects.
